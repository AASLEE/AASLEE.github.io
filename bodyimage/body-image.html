What to do when your body image is affecting your sex life. 

Negative thoughts about our body image are totally normal, but they’re not fun. Here are some tips for combatting negative body image when it comes to sex:

Focus on your breathing, your partner’s movements, and the pleasurable sensations you’re feeling.

Try mindful sex

Don’t feel like you have to perform

Not every position is going to be aesthetically pleasing, but who cares when it feels good?

Follow people on social media who look like you

Curating our feeds to feature a range of bodies is a very simple way to re-tune our brains and shift what we define as sexy.

Remember: the person you’re in bed with wants to be there

In the same way that you desire whoever you’re having sex with, try to remember that they want you just as much.

Separate your thoughts from reality

Just because we’re feeling something, doesn’t mean it’s objectively true. Self-confidence is a muscle; something we all need to flex and strengthen from time to time.
